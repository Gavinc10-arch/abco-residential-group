Privacy Policy

How ABCO Residential Group collects, uses and protects information submitted through this website.

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ABCO Residential Group collects, uses and protects information submitted through our website and related communications.

1. Who we are

ABCO Residential Group is a residential property investment and redevelopment brand focused on Austin, Texas. We use this website to provide information about our business, projects, partnerships and property acquisition services.

For privacy questions, contact us at contact@abcoresidential.com.

2. Information we may collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when you complete a form, contact us, request a cash offer, discuss a property, or ask about working with us.

  • Your name, phone number and email address.
  • Property address and property-related information.
  • Details you provide about your circumstances or reason for contacting us.
  • Messages, enquiries and communications sent to us.
  • Basic website usage information, such as pages viewed, browser type and referral source, where analytics tools are used.

3. How we use your information

We use information for legitimate business purposes, including to respond to enquiries, assess property opportunities, arrange calls or meetings, communicate with sellers, brokers, investors, lenders and partners, improve our website and comply with legal, tax, regulatory, accounting or record-keeping obligations.

4. Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information where reasonably necessary with professional advisers, title companies, lenders, contractors, agents, technology providers or other parties involved in assessing or completing a property transaction.

We may also disclose information where required by law, regulation, court order or to protect our rights, property, users or business.

5. Cookies and analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to understand website performance, improve user experience and support marketing activity. If tools such as Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager or Meta Pixel are activated, they may collect usage information in line with their own policies. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect information provided to us. However, no website, email system or internet transmission is completely secure. We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, tax, accounting and record-keeping purposes.

7. Your choices

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you, subject to legal and business record-keeping requirements.

8. Third-party links

Our website may link to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
